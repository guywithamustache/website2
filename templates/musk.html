Elon Musk

Elon musk was raised in Pretoria, South Africa. His mother a model, his father an engineer. At age 10 he became interested in computers and video games and began to write his own code. He was bullied in school and had a rough life at home. He eventually made his way by himself to Canada, from there to America. He started his own internet company with his brother, reinvested the money into a rockey comapany, SpaceX, and an electric car company, Tesla.
